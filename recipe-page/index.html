Super-Healthy Breakfast Porridge

Fancy porridge made by a lazy cook.
Let's get cooking.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • A little over half a pot of water (in 36-quart pot)
  • 2 packs of cut ribs
  • 1 spoon full of salt
  • 2 sticks of green onion

Procedure

Prepare the porridge

  1. Use a 36-quart cooking pot, and fill it with a little over half amount of water.
  2. Measure 2 cups of rice; wash and drain it.
  3. Wait until water boils, then add rice into the pot.
  4. After adding rice, put pot on medium to low heat for about an hour.

Prepare the extra porridge goodies

  1. Unpack, wash, and cut raw ribs into little cubes.
  2. Boil ribs in a separate pot for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Drain ribs from water, and pour ribs into the main 36-quart pot.
  4. Chop and cube 2 sticks of green onion; set aside for last part of cooking process

Wait for a while...

  1. Add a spoon full of salt 30 minutes into porridge-boiling process.

Add the Green Onions

  1. About 50 minutes into the porridge-boiling process, add the green onions into the 36-quart pot.

Done.
